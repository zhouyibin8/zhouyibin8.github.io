The Art Institute of Chicago

📍 Chicago, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2024.3

The Art Institute of Chicago is one of the most famous art museums in the world, with nearly 300,000 works in its collection, covering thousands of years of art history.


Grant Wood's "American Gothic", Georges Seurat's "A Sunday on La Grande Jatte", Edward Hopper's "Nighthawks", Vincent van Gogh's "The Bedroom"...

The Field Museum

📍 Chicago, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2024.3

The Field Museum is one of the leading natural history museums in the world, known for its vast collection and contributions to research.


Fossil of Sue the T. rex, Egyptian mummies, the "Green Star" meteorite...

Royal Ontario Museum

📍 Toronto, Canada 🇨🇦 ｜ 2024.3

The Royal Ontario Museum is one of the largest and most historic museums in Canada, known for its comprehensive collections and diverse exhibitions.


Dinosaur fossils, Egyptian mummies, Han Dynasty burial sculptures from China, artifacts from Canada's First Nations, and medieval European armor and weapons...

Philadelphia Museum of Art

📍 Philadelphia, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2023.12

The Philadelphia Museum of Art is one of the largest art museums, known for its diverse collection of art and historical artifacts.


Vincent van Gogh's "Sunflowers", Renoir's "The Bathers", Cézanne's "Mont Sainte-Victoire"...

Museum of Fine Arts, Boston

📍 Boston, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2023.12

The Museum of Fine Arts, Boston, housing a collection of fine arts from around the world, showcasing a rich cultural heritage from ancient to modern art.


Yan Liben's "Portraits of Emperors", Katsushika Hokusai's "The Great Wave off Kanagawa"...

MIT Museum

📍 Boston, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2023.12

The MIT Museum is a museum dedicated to showcasing technology and innovation, committed to demonstrating the development and impact of science and technology.


The world's first digital computer, early computer art, robotics technology, achievements in artificial intelligence research...

Harvard Art Museums

📍 Boston, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2023.12

The Harvard Art Museums are an integral part of Harvard University. They possess a rich collection of artworks from around the world, showcasing various forms of art including paintings, sculptures, and photography.


Vincent van Gogh's "The Starry Night", Leonardo da Vinci's "Mona Lisa", and Pablo Picasso's "The Guitarist"...

American Museum of Natural History

📍 New York, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2023.12

Located in New York City, the American Museum of Natural History displays the natural world and human culture. It served as the primary filming location for the movie "Night at the Museum".

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

📍 New York, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2023.12

The MET is one of the four major museums in the world, located in New York City. It houses a diverse collection of art pieces and artifacts, providing an in-depth look at history and culture to its visitors.


The Temple of Dendur, Yuan Dynasty murals of the "Medicine Buddha", Vincent van Gogh's "Self-Portrait"...

Yale University Art Gallery

📍 New Haven, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2023.12

The Yale University Art Gallery is the oldest university art museum in the Western Hemisphere.


Van Gogh's "Night coffee shop"...

Museum of Modern Arts

📍 New York, US 🇺🇸 ｜ 2023.8

MoMA was founded in 1929 and is recognized along with Tate Modern and Tate Britain and the National Georges Pompidou Centre of Art and Culture as the most important modern art museums.


Van Gogh's "Starry Night", Monet's "Water Lilies"...

National Museum of Bangkok

📍 BKK, TH 🇹🇭 ｜ 2023.6

The Bangkok National Museum is located in the capital of Thailand, Bangkok, and is the largest museum in the country.


It was established in 1874, originally to showcase the private collection of King Rama V of Thailand.
