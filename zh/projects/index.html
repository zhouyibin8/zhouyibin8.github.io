Unveiling Customer Engagement in Destination Marketing: A Multimodal Approach to Analyzing Official Promotional Videos#

Authors: Yibin Berry Zhou, Chen Jason Zhang, Menglong Xia#

Journal: International Journal of Tourism Research

Key Findings & Contributions:

  • Dual-Pathway Framework: Developed a theoretical framework integrating cognitive load, elaboration likelihood, and media richness theories.
  • Multimodal Analysis: Utilized computer vision and LightGBM with SHAP to analyze 5,037 Instagram videos from U.S. national parks.
  • Engagement Drivers: Found that simpler, familiar content drives passive likes, while emotionally resonant, high-aesthetic content fosters active comments.
  • Visual Cues: Identified that thumbnailâ€“video contrasts and color coherence are critical predictors of customer engagement.
This study examines how features of official destination promotional videos (DPVs) shape customer engagement (CE) on social media. Using 5,037 Instagram videos from 54 U.S. national parks, we apply machine learning and computer vision to extract video content, visual features, multimodal cues (text, audio, thumbnails), and post metadata. LightGBM with SHAP analyses shows that thumbnailâ€“video contrasts, color coherence, and emotional cues significantly influence CE. Simpler, familiar content drives passive engagement (likes), whereas emotionally resonant, high-aesthetic content fosters active engagement (comments). Beyond method, this study contributes a dual-pathway theoretical framework that integrates cognitive load, elaboration likelihood, and media richness theories to explain how multimodal features jointly shape CE. Practical implications highlight balancing content simplicity and depth, ensuring visual and emotional consistency, and optimizing posting strategies. Overall, this research advances destination marketing by providing a scalable and theory-informed framework for analyzing social media videos.

Zhou, Y. B., Zhang, C. J., & Xia, M. (2025). Unveiling customer engagement in destination marketing: A multimodal approach to analyzing official promotional videos. International Journal of Tourism Research.
